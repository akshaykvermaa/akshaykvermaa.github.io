Work

  • 2022.12 - Present

    Sheffield, UK

    Research Associate (III–V Epitaxy, MBE)
    National Epitaxy Facility, University of Sheffield
    MBE process and development engineer for III–V quantum photonic and optoelectronic structures.
    • Operate and develop processes on a DCA Dual-P700 MBE reactor for GaAs, AlGaAs, InGaAs, GaSb and related heterostructures.
    • Delivered >100 wafers for internal projects, UK users and industry partners with >95% reproducibility.
